Provo River Rafting

Our goal is to provide fun, wholesome, family adventures. We have a range of adventures for every family. From slow, pleasant river trips down the peaceful Provo River, to white-water adventures down the Green River. We have every adventure for every type of family.

History

In 2004 The Eaton family had the opportunity to go on a river run down the Payette River. We rented a boat and all of the gear that we thought we would need, and headed up the canyon to begin our adventure. After many mishaps, including several members of the family ending up in the river, we decided that we should have hired a professional who could have helped us with the ins and outs of river running. Despite the difficulties we encountered, we had such a great time that we wanted to make this kind of adventure more easily available to other families. And so, Provo River Rafting was born and the real adventures began!
